About Khumo EMS

Provincial care, built on local response.

Khumo Emergency Medical Services is a licensed private healthcare provider operating across Gauteng, holding operating licenses for Intermediate Life Support and event medical coverage. We run three bases — in Grobler Park, Mondeor, and Bezuidenhout Valley — so that response times stay short whether you're in Johannesburg, Roodepoort or Vosloorus.

Our crews handle everything from routine patient transport between facilities to full on-site medical standby at events, and emergency response care when it matters most. Dispatch is answered around the clock.

What we do

Three core services, one standard of care.

Patient Transport

Scheduled and inter-facility transport for patients who need monitored, professional care in transit — from routine transfers to higher-acuity moves.

Event Medical Standby

On-site medical cover for sporting events, festivals, corporate functions and gatherings of any size, staffed to the risk profile of your event.

Emergency Response

Rapid-dispatch Intermediate Life Support care for emergencies, backed by crews stationed across three Gauteng bases for faster arrival.
